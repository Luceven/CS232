    Shefali Kumar received her undergraduate degree in Biology at Columbia University in New York City. At Columbia, she was on the Undergraduate Recruitment Committee where she worked with prospective students and the Office of Undergraduate Admissions. For the past three years she has served on Columbia University's Alumni Representative Committee where she interviews and reviews prospective students seeking undergraduate admission to Columbia University. For five years Shefali has also tutored and reviewed college application materials for students in the Greater New York area. Shefali holds a Master of Public Health degree with a concentration in Health Policy from the Yale School of Public Health. Currently Shefali works at a healthcare consulting firm in the San Francisco Bay Area.
